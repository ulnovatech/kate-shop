# Kate Admin — Play Store listing (draft)

**App name:** Kate Admin  
**Short description:** Staff operations for Kate Shop — orders, catalog, and team tools.  
**Full description:**

Kate Admin is the staff companion app for Kate Shop. Sign in with your team invite, manage orders on the go, and stay notified when customers place new orders.

Features:

- Order inbox and fulfillment workflow
- Catalog and inventory tools (role-based)
- Secure staff authentication
- Push alerts for new orders (when enabled)

Kate Admin requires an active staff account. Customers should use the Kate Shop storefront app or website.
